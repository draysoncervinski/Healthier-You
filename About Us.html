About Us

This website was created as the capstone project for my Bachelor's of Integrated Studies through Weber State University. I wanted to showcase what I have learned in my three areas of study: Computer Science, Health Administrative Services and Nutrition.

Losing weight can be an overwhelming process, especially with all of the FAD diets out there. It does not need to be overly complicated. By becoming concious of what you eat and monitoring calories and macronutrients you can make big changes to your life.

The main purpose of this website was to help others lose weight in a healthy, sustainable manner and to provide easy to follow recipes to help them on their weight loss journey. I also wanted to show the coorelations between nutrition, health, and potentially associated health care costs from not monitoring weight and health.
