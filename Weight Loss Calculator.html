The Weight Loss Calculator

The calculator below is designed to help you lose weight by providing you with your suggested daily caloric and macronutrient needs based upon your wieight loss goal. Want to maintain your current weight? It can help with that too. Just input your information, and the calculator will give you your personalized results.

The Mayo Clinic recommends a healthy weight loss of 1-2 pounds per week. In order to acheive this, they recommend being in a calorie defecit of 500 - 1,000 calories per day (500 calorie deficit for 1 lb and 1,000 calorie deficit for 2 lbs). The formula that the calculator uses is based on these recommendations.

The Institute of Medicine of the National Academies recommend that people get 45–65% of their calories from carbs, 20–35% of their calories from fats and 10–35% of their calories from proteins. The calculator below will distibute your calorie needs as follows: 50% carbs, 30% protein, and 20% from fats.

Added Sugars:

  • Men: The AHA suggests an added-sugar limit of no more than 150 calories per day (about 9 teaspoons or 36 grams of sugar).
  • Women: The AHA suggests an added-sugar limit of no more than 100 calories per day (about 6 teaspoons or 24 grams of sugar).
