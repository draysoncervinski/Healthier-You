Common Macros and Easy Recipes

Monitoring calorie and macronutrient intakes can be extremely monotonous. Below you will find a table with 25 commonly eaten healthy foods. At the bottom of the page there are links to easy and healthy recipes that can be followed from anywhere, even a dorm! If the food you are trying to track is not on the table, click here and you can look up the nutrition facts for any food you'd like! This website is also a great way to keep track of what you eat throughout the day. You just log the foods you eat, and it keeps track of everything for you!

Tip: Invest in a digital kitchen scale. Not only does it make portioning foods easier. It is also the most accurate way to measure out your portions! Here is a link to the food scale that I use. I have had mine for over three years and have ZERO complaints!
